# Talking about your job

## Questions


About the Company 

1.      What is the name of the company you work for? 

_My company's name is "ICT Berufsbildungscenter" or abbreviated "ICT BBC"_

2.      Can you describe what your company does? 

- _Our company offers the basic apprenticeship year for its partner companies_

3.      How long has the company been in business? 

4.      What do you think makes your company unique in the IT industry? 

5.      How would you describe the company culture? 

Role and Responsibilities 

1.      What is your job title? 

2.      Can you describe your primary responsibilities? 

3.      What projects are you currently working on? 

4.      How does your role fit into the overall goals of the company? 

5.      What skills do you use most in your job? 

Experiences in the Job 

1.      How long have you been working in this role? 

2.      What was your first day at work like? 

3.      Can you share a memorable experience you've had at work? 

4.      Have you faced any significant challenges in your job? How did you overcome them? 

5.      What have you learned since starting your apprenticeship? 

Colleagues and Team Dynamics 

1.      How many people are in your team? 

2.      Can you describe the people you work with? 

3.      How do you and your colleagues support each other? 

4.      Have you worked on any team projects? What was that like? 

5.      What is your favourite thing about your team? 

A Typical Day 

1.      What does a typical day at work look like for you? 

2.      How do you start your workday? 

3.      What are the busiest times of your day? 

4.      Do you have regular meetings or check-ins? 

5.      How do you usually end your workday? 

Likes, Dislikes, and Challenges 

1.      What do you enjoy most about your job? 

2.      Is there anything about your job that you find difficult or frustrating? 

3.      What has been the most rewarding part of your job? 

4.      Are there any tasks you don't enjoy? Why? 

5.      How do you deal with difficult situations or tasks at work? 

Skills and Development 

1.      What skills have you developed since starting your apprenticeship? 

2.      How do you keep up with new developments in IT? 

3.      Are there any skills you want to improve or learn? 

4.      How does your company support your professional development? 

5.      What are your career goals for the next few years? 

Work-Life Balance and Personal Insights 

1.      How do you balance work with your personal life? 

2.      What hobbies or activities do you enjoy outside of work? 

3.      Has working in IT changed your perspective on technology in everyday life? 

4.      What advice would you give to someone considering a career in IT? 

5.      How has your apprenticeship impacted your personal growth?
