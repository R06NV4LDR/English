English File 1B test content:
-Question tags
(e.g. "You don't like apples, do you?")
-Reply questions
(e.g. A: "I went to Portugal last week"
B: "Did you? I thought you went to Spain!")
-Using the auxiliary for emphasis
(e.g. A: "I am the best in the class"
B: "You aren't! You don’t even turn up to class half the time.")
-Expressing similarity/ difference
(e.g. A: "I love summer!"
B: "Me too!" ---> Similarity in positive sentences
A: "I don't enjoy winter."
B: "Me neither!" ---> Similarity in negative sentences
A: "I really like going to Christmas markets."
B: "I don't!" ---> Difference
A: "I don't like Christmas holidays"
B: "I do!" ---> Difference
-Reading comprehension
