    **Lesson 2**
    
    ⫸ Set up for the test
    
    - Sit on the opposite side of the table (with your back towards me, facing the back of the classroom)
    - Screen brightness up high
    - You can listen to music during the test BUT you cannot touch your phone during the test, so choose now what you want to listen to.
    - Phones away or face down on the table
    - When the test starts there should be NO speaking
    - When you have finished tell me, but don't communicate with anyone else
    
    ⫸ Complete test
